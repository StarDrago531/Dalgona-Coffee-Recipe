☕Dalgona Coffee Recipe☕

This Yummy South Korean Coffee Treat that's all over the Internet only requires Four ingredients to make - All of which you probably have in the Cupboard – You feel like you’ve won the Jackpot, Right?

Ingredients

  • Instant Coffee
  • Sugar
  • Hot Water
  • Milk

Dalgona Making Process

Step 1: Combine The Instant Coffee, Sugar, Hot Water
Add 2 Tablespoons (tbsp) of Instant Coffee, 2 tbsp of Sugar, 2 tbsp of Hot Water to a medium mixing Bowl
Step 2: Whisk, Whisk, Whisk until Coffee is light and Fluffy
Using a electric Hand Held Whisker, Whip the Coffee mixture until it is light brown and Fluffy, and holds stiff peaks when whisk is removed
Step 3: Serve with milk:
Use a spoon to Granish a glass of Hot or Cold Milk with Coffee Cream, Serve with straw, and stir, stir, stir to enjoy!
